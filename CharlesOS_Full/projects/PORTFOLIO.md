# Portfolio

## Portfolio Principle

The portfolio should show Charles as a strategic, commercially minded Principal Product Designer who connects design craft to measurable business outcomes.

## Preferred Case Study Structure

1. Business impact
2. Problem
3. Context
4. Insight
5. Decision-making
6. Design response
7. Trade-offs
8. Outcome
9. What this proves about Charles

## Style

- Punchy
- Business-first
- Senior
- Evidence-led
- Avoid overly long UX process explanations
- Show judgement, not just artefacts
