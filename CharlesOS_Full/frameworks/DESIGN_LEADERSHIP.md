# Design Leadership Framework

## Core Principle

Design leadership is about creating clarity, raising quality and connecting design work to business and customer outcomes.

## Leadership Areas

### Strategy

- Define the problem
- Connect design to business outcomes
- Set direction
- Make choices visible

### Craft

- Raise quality
- Simplify complexity
- Improve usability
- Protect accessibility

### Systems

- Create reusable patterns
- Reduce duplicated work
- Improve consistency
- Make better work easier

### Influence

- Align stakeholders
- Explain trade-offs
- Bring people with you
- Make decisions easier

### Coaching

- Help people improve judgement
- Give useful feedback
- Build confidence
- Create clarity

## Charles' Leadership Style

- Direct
- Practical
- Outcome-focused
- Calm under ambiguity
- Strong on systems thinking
- High standards for craft
- Commercially aware
