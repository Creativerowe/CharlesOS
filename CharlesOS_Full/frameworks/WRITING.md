# Writing Framework

## Style Rules

- No em dashes
- Use % symbol
- Avoid the word "foster"
- Be clear
- Be direct
- Avoid bloated corporate language
- Prefer short paragraphs
- Use strong opening lines
- Avoid clichés
- Keep the reader moving

## Preferred Tone

- Opinionated
- Practical
- Reflective
- Experienced
- Human
- Commercially aware

## Article Structure

1. Strong opening
2. Clear problem
3. Personal observation
4. Broader industry point
5. Practical insight
6. Strong ending

## Portfolio Writing Structure

1. Outcome
2. Problem
3. Action
4. Trade-off
5. Result
6. What it proves

## Avoid

- Generic thought leadership
- Buzzwords without substance
- Long abstract intros
- Overexplaining process
- Weak endings
