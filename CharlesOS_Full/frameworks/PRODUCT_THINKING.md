# Product Thinking Framework

## Core Principle

Great product thinking connects customer need, business value, technical feasibility and measurable outcomes.

## Framework

1. Problem
2. Customer
3. Business outcome
4. Constraints
5. Insight
6. Options
7. Trade-offs
8. Decision
9. Measurement
10. Learning

## Questions to Ask

- What problem are we solving?
- Who has the problem?
- Why does it matter now?
- What business metric does this affect?
- What evidence do we have?
- What are the constraints?
- What would success look like?
- What are the risks?
- What is the simplest valuable version?
- How will we know if it worked?

## Output Style

Recommendations should be:

- Practical
- Prioritised
- Commercially aware
- Customer-centred
- Clear about trade-offs
